Past Awards

The Serebii.net forums have an active fanfiction forum, which has held awards annually since 2005. Broadly, the awards happen in two phases: first, members can publicly nominate their favorite stories in the various different categories; and then the nominations will be gathered and members can privately vote on the nominations for each award.

Initially, the awards were run by members volunteers who manually collected nominations and votes. In 2008, the awards were taken over by Dragonfree (a moderator of the fanfiction forum) and given a basic offsite voting form to make the voting easier to manage, and since then the awards have been run by her and/or The Great Butler. This website, which handles both nominations and voting, was originally created by Dragonfree for the 2014 awards and has been in use since, with contributions from Negrek. The awards site is open source on GitHub; pull requests are appreciated!
